Discord Duplicati Notifications

James Lloyd

Nov 7, 2022 1 min

Out the box, duplicati has limited notification options and appears to lean towards pushing you down the email route. I really wanted to have my backup notifications in discord and I wanted a way to graph the number of files in the backup specifically.  As for a quick and dirty check if the number goes up, its likely backing up my photos etc.

So I wrote a little script to utilise the send-http-url report advanced option. To give notifications that look like the below

How to

  1. Setup up your desired Discord webhook (https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/228383668-Intro-to-Webhooks)
  2. Copy your webhook url
  3. Goto https://duplicati-notifications.lloyd.ws
  4. Enter the name of your backup (this is completely arbitrary, but will be used to identify stats later on)
  5. Paste your Webhook url into the webhook field
  6. Click "Create send-http-url URL"
  7. Copy the generated url
  8. Goto Duplicati and edit your already working backup job.
  9. In section 5 at the bottom from the dropbox box select send-http-url
  10. in the send-http-url form box that will now appear paste in the url from  https://duplicati-notifications.lloyd.ws
  11. Save your backup job.
  12. Done!
